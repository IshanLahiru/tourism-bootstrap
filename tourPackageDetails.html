Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our 5-Day Enchanted Forest Discovery tour.

What is included in the tour package?

Our 5-Day Enchanted Forest Discovery package includes:

  • Professional English-speaking guide
  • All accommodation in eco-lodges
  • All meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner)
  • Transportation throughout the tour
  • Forest reserve entrance fees
  • All activities and workshops
  • Airport transfers

What should I pack for this tour?

Essential items to pack include:

  • Comfortable hiking shoes or boots
  • Lightweight, breathable clothing
  • Rain jacket or poncho
  • Insect repellent
  • Sunscreen and hat
  • Water bottle
  • Camera and binoculars
  • Personal toiletries

What is the difficulty level of the activities?

This tour is designed for moderate fitness levels:

  • Forest treks are 2-4 hours with gentle to moderate inclines
  • Canopy walks are suitable for most ages (minimum height requirement applies)
  • Activities can be adjusted for different fitness levels
  • No previous hiking experience required
  • Professional guides ensure safety and comfort

What is the best time to visit Sinharaja Forest?

The best time to visit Sinharaja Forest Reserve is:

  • Dry Season (January to April): Best for hiking and wildlife spotting
  • Monsoon Season (May to December): Lush greenery and fewer crowds
  • Peak Wildlife Viewing: Early morning (6-9 AM) and late afternoon (4-6 PM)
  • Bird Watching: Year-round, with migratory species from November to March

Are there any age restrictions for this tour?

Age requirements and recommendations:

  • Minimum Age: 8 years old for most activities
  • Canopy Walk: Minimum height of 120cm (4 feet)
  • Children 8-12: Must be accompanied by adults
  • Teenagers: Can participate in all activities
  • Senior Travelers: Welcome with moderate fitness level

What happens in case of bad weather?

We have flexible plans for weather conditions:

  • Light rain: Tour continues with provided rain gear
  • Heavy rain: Alternative indoor activities and cultural experiences
  • Severe weather: Tour rescheduling or full refund options
  • Real-time weather monitoring by our guides
  • Backup plans always available

How do I book this tour and what are the payment options?

Booking and payment information:

  • Booking: Online through our website or contact us directly
  • Deposit: 30% required to confirm booking
  • Payment Methods: Credit card, bank transfer, or PayPal
  • Full Payment: Due 30 days before tour start
  • Cancellation Policy: Full refund up to 14 days before tour
