Top Attractions in Sri Lanka

Discover the most breathtaking destinations and iconic landmarks

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

The ancient palace and fortress complex of Sigiriya is one of Sri Lanka's most iconic landmarks. This UNESCO World Heritage site features stunning frescoes, landscaped gardens, and breathtaking views from the summit.
